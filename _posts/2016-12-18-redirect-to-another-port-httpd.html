Create virtual host, example

<virtualhost *:80>
ProxyPreserveHost On
ProxyRequests Off
ServerName downloads.wajatmaka.com
ServerAlias wajatmaka.com
ProxyPass / http://localhost:8080/
ProxyPassReverse / http://localhost:8080/
<proxy>
Order deny,allow
Allow from all
</proxy>
</virtualhost>
